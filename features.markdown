SentinelIS is a feature-rich application. All features are carefully planned and implemented to integrate seamlessly into the system. If you are looking for a feature that could benefit not only your organization, feel free to open an issue or implement it yourself. For more information on contributing, see the contribution guidelines.

## Mimo Chatbot

Mimo is your friendly assistant, ready to help you with the setup and any questions that may arise while using SentinelIS. Mimo does not have AI integration; instead, he uses predefined questions and answers. While he may not be able to address every question in full detail, he will always try his best to assist you with any concern.
